Conspiracy Hunter

Suspicious objects — birds, UFOs, greys, hats, and more — will start appearing around the screen. Click (or tap) each one before it vanishes to score a point — chain hits in a row to build a combo multiplier. Miss one and it costs you a point and breaks your combo.

Watch out for the bomb — click it and you lose 5 points (and your combo)! Leave it alone and it fizzles out safely.

You have 60 seconds. Good luck, agent.
